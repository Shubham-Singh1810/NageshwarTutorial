FIRST STEP

Aims & Objectives :

››Gradually enhances the level of student from Ground Level to Top Level.

›› Scoring high marks in BOARD Examination.

››Top Ranks in JEE (MAIN & ADVANCED), KVPY, SCIENCE OLYMPIAD, MATH OLYMPIAD & other State and National Level Engineering Entrance Examinations.

Eligibility :

››For Class 11 and 12 going students.

Course Description :

First Step is the course designed for students moving from class X to X1. It is the initial phase of preparation and aims at building story foundation for JEE( MAIN & ADVANCED) / NEET & AIIMS Exams.

    Main Features
  • Small Batch, Doubt Class.
  • Special Care for weak students
  • Chapterwise Test
  • Special attention to :-
  • Mole Concept
  • Balancing of redox reaction
  • Organic reaction mechanism, conversions.
  • Numerical solving technique using log table
